Wise Old Man Competition

How to Use BinGods

  • The Board — Each tile is a bingo challenge. Tiles glow in a team's color when completed. Gold glow means both teams completed it.
  • Click a Tile — Opens a detail panel showing the challenge description, point value, and completion status.
  • Multi-Item Tiles — Some tiles require multiple items. These show a progress badge (e.g. 2/4) on the board, and a per-team checklist when clicked.
  • Team Effort Tiles — Tiles like KC goals or XP targets show progress toward the target. Points are only awarded once the full goal is reached.
  • Team Panels — The side panels show each team's score, roster, in-progress tiles, and completed tiles. Click the team name to view all team effort stats with progress bars.
  • Unlocking God Tiles — To unlock a god tier, complete the adjacent lock tiles. Click any tile to see what it unlocks or requires.
  • Wise Old Man Link — Opens the WOM competition tracker for live XP gains.

WOM Competition Tips

  • To preview different metrics, click the three dots menu (…) then select "Preview as…"

Screenshot Submissions

  • All screenshots submitted to your team's bingo Discord channel must show the codeword to count.
  • The easiest way is to install the Wise Old Man plugin on RuneLite, then in the plugin settings add the event codeword — it will display on your screen automatically.

Recommended OSRS Settings

  • Lower the loot notification threshold to 10k so smaller drops are captured in screenshots.
  • Enable untradeable drop notifications in your OSRS settings so unique drops are always shown.
