How to Use BinGods

  • The Board — Each tile is a bingo challenge. Tiles glow in a team's color when completed. Multiple teams can complete the same tile.
  • Click a Tile — Opens a detail panel showing the challenge description, requirements, point value, and which teams have completed it.
  • Team Panels — The side panels show each team's score and members. The captain is listed first.
  • Unlocking God Tiles — The board has tiers of god tiles. To unlock the next tier, you must complete the adjacent lock tiles. Click a lock tile to see which tiles are required to unlock it, and click a god tile to see which tiles it unlocks in the next tier.
  • Wise Old Man Link — Opens the WOM competition tracker for live XP gains.

WOM Competition Tips

  • To preview different metrics, click the three dots menu (…) then select "Preview as…"
